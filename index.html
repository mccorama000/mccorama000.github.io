About Me

I am a student at Eastern Washington University. I am currently on track to get my degree in Visual Communication Design, and a minor in Business. Coming to Eastern was not far from home for me, as I was born and raised in Spokane, Washington, and have lived in the same house since I was born. I discovered my passion for Graphic Design at the STEM school that I attended in high school, Riverpoint Academy.

I hope to one day work for a company where I can use my design skills and passions to meet the needs of the business and establish a unique identity through a variety of different ways.
